Bibliography
National Prosecutors Survey, 1996

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 2433. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. This list represents all items known
to ICPSR as of 2015-08-03.

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
bibliography@icpsr.umich.edu.


1. DeFrances, Carol J.; Steadman, Greg W., "Prosecutors in State Courts,
1996." NCJ 170092, United States Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice
Statistics, Jul 1998.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/psc96.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/psc96.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/psc96.pdf
Presents findings from the 1996 National Survey of Prosecutors, the latest
in series of biennial sample surveys of the Nation's 2,300 State court
prosecutors.

2. Harlow, Caroline Wolf, "Defense Counsel in Criminal Cases." Bureau of
Justice Statistics Special Report. NCJ 179023, Washington, DC: United
States Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics, Nov 2000.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/dccc.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/dccc.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/dccc.pdf
Examines issues of legal representation for defendants in Federal district
court and large local jurisdictions, and inmates in local jails and Federal
and State prison.

3. Owens, Emily G., "Media and the Criminal Justice System." Jun 2010..
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/dccc.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/dccc.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/dccc.pdf
Examines issues of legal representation for defendants in Federal district
court and large local jurisdictions, and inmates in local jails and Federal
and State prison.
