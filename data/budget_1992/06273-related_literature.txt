Bibliography
National Prosecutors Survey, 1992

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 6273. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. This list represents all items known
to ICPSR as of 2015-08-10.

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
bibliography@icpsr.umich.edu.


1. Davison, John M.; Smith, Steven K.; DeFrances, Carol J., "Prosecutors in
State Courts, 1992." NCJ 145319, United States Department of Justice,
Bureau of Justice Statistics, Dec 1993.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/pisc92.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/pisc92.txt
Presents findings from the 1992 National Prosecutor Survey Program, a
series of biennial sample surveys of the Nation's 2,300 State court
prosecutors.

2. Dawson, John M.; Smith, Steven K.; DeFrances, Carol J., "Prosecutors in
State Courts, 1992." Bulletin. NCJ 145319, Washington, DC: United States
Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics, Dec 1993.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/pisc92.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/pisc92.txt
Presents findings from the 1992 National Prosecutor Survey Program, a
series of biennial sample surveys of the Nation's 2,300 State court
prosecutors.

3. Smith, Steven K.; DeFrances, Carol J., "Indigent Defense." Bureau of
Justice Statistics Selected Findings. NCJ 158909, Washington, DC: United
States Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice Statistics, Feb 1996.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/id.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/id.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/id.pdf
This report of presented selected findings drawn from various BJS surveys
contains information related to the defense of indigent criminal
defendants.
