Bibliography
National Prosecutors Survey [Census], 2001

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 3418. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. This list represents all items known
to ICPSR as of 2015-08-03.

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
bibliography@icpsr.umich.edu.


1. Baicker, Katherine, "The budgetary repercussions of capital
convictions." Advances in Economic Analysis and Policy. Aug 2004, 4,. DOI:
10.2202/1538-0637.1311

2. Buzzell, Timothy, "The effects of organizational and community context
on local prosecution of computer child pornography cases." Criminal Justice
Studies. Dec 2007, 40, (4), 391 - 405. DOI: 10.1080/14786010701758179

3. Byers, Bryan D.; Warren-Gordon, Kiesha; Jones, James A., "Predictors of
hate crime prosecutions: An analysis of data from the National Prosecutors
Survey and state-level bias crime laws." Race and Justice. Jul 2012, 2,
(3), 203 - 219. DOI: 10.1177/2153368712446868

4. Cunningham, William Scott; Renauer, Brian C.; Khalifa, Christy, "Sharing

5. DeFrances, Carol J., "Prosecutors in State Courts, 2001." NCJ 193441,
Washington, DC: United States Department of Justice, Bureau of Justice
Statistics, May 2002.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/psc01.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/psc01.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/psc01.pdf
This report presents findings from the 2001 National Survey of Prosecutors,
which was a census of all 2,341 prosecutors' offices that tried felony
cases in State courts of general jurisdiction.

6. DeFrances, Carol J., "State Court Prosecutors in Large Districts, 2001."
NCJ 191206, Washington, DC: United States Department of Justice, Bureau of
Justice Statistics, Dec 2001.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/scpld01.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/scpld01.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/scpld01.pdf
This Bureau of Justice Statistics' report presents data from the 2001
National Survey of Prosecutors (NSP).

7. DeFrances, Carol J., "State Court Prosecutors in Small Districts, 2001."
NCJ 196020, Washington, DC: United States Department of Justice, Bureau of
Justice Statistics, Jan 2003.
Abstract: http://www.bjs.gov/content/abstract/scpsd01.htm
Full text: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/ascii/scpsd01.txt
Full text PDF: http://www.bjs.gov/content/pub/pdf/scpsd01.pdf
Presents results from the 2001 National Survey of Prosecutors (NSP), which
collected data on all chief prosecutors that handled felony cases in State
courts of general jurisdiction. The report covers prosecutor's offices that
served a district with less than 250,000 population.
